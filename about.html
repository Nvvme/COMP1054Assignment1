About This Song and Fallout

"I Don't Want To Set The World On Fire" by The Ink Spots holds a special place in the heart of all Fallout franchise fans. Its haunting melody capture the essence of the series' retro-futuristic, post-apocalyptic setting.

The song's inclusion in the Fallout series' soundtrack enhances the atmospheric nostalgia, enveloping players in a world that is both familiar and eerily alien. Its lyrics, seemingly innocuous, take on a new depth within the context of the game. The desire "not to set the world on fire" ironically contrasts with the nuclear devastation that defines the Fallout universe.
